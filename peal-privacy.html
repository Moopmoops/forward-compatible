Privacy Policy

Peal doesn't collect, sell, or share your data. We never will.

Your alarms, calendar, location, and any health data on your device always stays fully on your device. Turn on iCloud sync and it syncs through your own private Apple iCloud — we never see it, and we run no servers, so we can't receive it.

  • No account.
  • No analytics or tracking.
  • No ads.
  • No third-party data SDKs.

Permissions, and why

Alarms — to ring.

Calendar — read-only, to build reminders.

Location — location alarms and travel time.

Health / Sleep — optional, to learn your wake time.

Each is used only on-device, only for the feature you turn on.
